## Trigger Phrases

- "brand positioning", "personal brand strategy", "how should I position myself" → `brand-identity-designer`
- "LinkedIn profile", "optimize my LinkedIn", "LinkedIn content" → `brand-linkedin-specialist`
- "portfolio", "case study", "show my work" → `brand-portfolio-advisor`

## Typical Brand-Building Workflow

1. `brand-identity-designer` → defines who you are, who you're for, and what you stand for
2. `brand-story-architect` → builds the narrative that brings the identity to life
3. `brand-linkedin-specialist` → optimizes the most important professional platform
4. `brand-portfolio-advisor` → curates proof of the claims you're making
5. `brand-visibility-planner` → builds the reach and recognition strategy
