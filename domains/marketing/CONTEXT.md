## How Skills Interoperate

A typical workflow:
1. `mkt-strategist` → defines positioning, ICP, messaging pillars
2. `mkt-campaign-planner` → builds the campaign structure from strategy
3. `mkt-copywriter` → writes the actual copy for each campaign asset
4. `mkt-email-marketer` → builds nurture sequences for leads generated
5. `mkt-seo-specialist` → ensures content ranks and attracts organic traffic
6. `mkt-analytics-advisor` → measures, attributes, and optimizes everything
