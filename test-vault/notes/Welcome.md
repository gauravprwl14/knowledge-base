# Welcome to KMS

KMS (Knowledge Management System) helps you find, search, and understand your documents.

## Features
- Google Drive integration
- Obsidian vault scanning
- Semantic search powered by BGE-M3
- Document summarisation

## Getting Started
1. Connect your Google Drive or Obsidian vault
2. Run a scan
3. Search your documents
